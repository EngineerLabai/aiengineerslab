## Calculation Explanation

<Callout type="info" title="How to use?">
<StepList title="Quick steps" steps={["Pick a category and units", "Enter the value", "Read and copy the converted result"]} />
</Callout>

<Formula label="General conversion">
V_out = V_in × factor_from / factor_to
</Formula>

The tool converts the selected unit to the category base unit (Pa, N, m, J) and then back to the target unit.

**Assumptions**
- Linear conversion factors only (no temperature or non-linear conversions).
- SI base units are used as the reference.

**Units**
- Length: m (mm, cm, km, in, ft)
- Force: N (kN, lbf)
- Pressure: Pa (kPa, MPa, bar, psi)
- Energy: J (kJ, MJ, Wh, kWh, cal)

**References**
- ISO 80000 (Quantities and units)
- NIST conversion factors

<Callout type="warning" title="Out of scope / Warning">
Temperature conversions (°C/°F), gas volume corrections, and property conversions (density, viscosity) are not covered.
</Callout>
