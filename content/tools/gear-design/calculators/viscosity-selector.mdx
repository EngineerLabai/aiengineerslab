## Calculation Explanation

<Callout type="info" title="How to use?">
<StepList title="Quick steps" steps={["Enter ks and pitch line speed", "Select lubrication method and temperature", "Read the ISO VG recommendation"]} />
</Callout>

<Formula label="Selection factor">
Factor = ks / v
</Formula>

The tool maps the factor and operating conditions to a practical ISO VG grade and shows a neighboring range.

**Assumptions**
- Heuristic selector based on ks/v guidelines.
- Temperature and method apply small adjustments.

**Units**
- ks: N/mm
- v: m/s
- Temperature: °C

**References**
- ISO 3448 (ISO viscosity grades)
- AGMA 9005 (Lubrication guidelines)

<Callout type="warning" title="Out of scope / Warning">
This is a quick selector, not a substitute for OEM charts. Always verify with gearbox and oil supplier data.
</Callout>

## Examples

### Example 1: Splash lubrication

**Inputs**
- ks = 25 N/mm
- v = 12 m/s
- Method: splash
- T = 70 °C

**Outputs**
- Factor = 2.08
- Recommended: VG 100 (range 68–150)

### Example 2: Bath lubrication, high load

**Inputs**
- ks = 45 N/mm
- v = 4 m/s
- Method: bath
- T = 90 °C

**Outputs**
- Factor = 11.25
- Recommended: VG 460 (range 320–680)
