## Calculation Explanation

<Callout type="info" title="How to use?">
<StepList title="Quick steps" steps={["Enter pitch diameter and tooth count", "Read the raw module", "Compare with the suggested standard module"]} />
</Callout>

<Formula label="Module">
m = d / z
p = π · m
</Formula>

**Assumptions**
- Standard ISO modules list is used for suggestion.
- No profile shift; reference diameter is used.

**Units**
- d: mm
- m, p: mm

**References**
- ISO 54 (Standard modules)
- ISO 53 (Reference profile for involute gears)

<Callout type="warning" title="Out of scope / Warning">
Undercut limits and profile shift are not evaluated. For low tooth counts, check undercut and correction factors.
</Callout>

## Examples

### Example 1: Simple module

**Inputs**
- d = 200 mm
- z = 40

**Outputs**
- m = 5.000 mm
- p = 15.708 mm
- Suggested m = 5
- Suggested d = 200 mm

### Example 2: Off-standard diameter

**Inputs**
- d = 180 mm
- z = 44

**Outputs**
- m = 4.091 mm
- p = 12.852 mm
- Suggested m = 4
- Suggested d = 176 mm
- Deviation = -2.2%
