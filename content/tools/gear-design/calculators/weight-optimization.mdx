## Calculation Explanation

<Callout type="info" title="How to use?">
<StepList title="Quick steps" steps={["Enter outer diameter, bore, and face width", "Set material density and lighten factor", "Read solid and lightened mass"]} />
</Callout>

<Formula label="Mass estimate">
V = (π/4)(D² − d²) · b
m = V · 1e-9 · ρ
m_light = m · (1 − lighten)
</Formula>

**Assumptions**
- Gear body is modeled as a hollow cylinder.
- Lighten factor removes a uniform fraction of mass.

**Units**
- D, d, b: mm
- ρ: kg/m³
- Mass: kg

**References**
- Machine design handbooks (gear blank mass estimates)

<Callout type="warning" title="Out of scope / Warning">
Webs, spokes, keyways, and holes are not modeled. Structural checks and balance are required for final design.
</Callout>

## Examples

### Example 1: Steel gear

**Inputs**
- D = 250 mm, d = 60 mm
- b = 40 mm, ρ = 7850 kg/m³
- Lighten = 0.20

**Outputs**
- Solid mass = 14.53 kg
- Lightened mass = 11.62 kg
- Volume = 0.00185 m³

### Example 2: Aluminum gear

**Inputs**
- D = 200 mm, d = 50 mm
- b = 30 mm, ρ = 2700 kg/m³
- Lighten = 0.30

**Outputs**
- Solid mass = 2.39 kg
- Lightened mass = 1.67 kg
- Volume = 0.000884 m³
