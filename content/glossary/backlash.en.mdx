Backlash describes the amount of clearance between mating gear teeth. This clearance is needed for thermal expansion and assembly tolerances.

<Formula label="Simple approach">
Backlash = total clearance / number of teeth
</Formula>

<Callout type="success" title="Tips">
Higher backlash can increase noise and vibration. Very low backlash can stress lubrication conditions.
</Callout>
